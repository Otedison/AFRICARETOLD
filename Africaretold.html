About Africa Retold

Our Vision for a United Africa

Africa Retold is a movement dedicated to the principles of Pan-Africanism, advocating for unity, self-determination, and the collective advancement of African people worldwide.

We believe in an Africa that controls its own destiny, where leaders are accountable to their people, and where the continent's rich resources benefit all its citizens.

Our organization was founded by a diverse group of African intellectuals, activists, and diaspora members committed to rewriting Africa's narrative from one of exploitation to one of empowerment.

Our Core Mission

Pan-African Unity

Promoting solidarity among all people of African descent worldwide and fostering a sense of shared identity and purpose.

Accountable Leadership

Advocating for transparent, ethical governance and holding leaders accountable to their citizens.

Education & Empowerment

Providing educational resources and platforms to empower Africans to shape their own future.
